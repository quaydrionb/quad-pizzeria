About

Our Story

Founded in 2022, Quad Pizzeria was born out of a passion for pizza and a desire to break away from the ordinary. We believe that every meal should be an experience, and what better way to make that happen than with our innovative square pizzas?

Our Mission

At Quad Pizzeria, our mission is simple - to deliver delicious and unique square pizzas that bring joy to your taste buds. We are committed to using only the freshest ingredients, ensuring that each bite is a moment of culinary delight.
